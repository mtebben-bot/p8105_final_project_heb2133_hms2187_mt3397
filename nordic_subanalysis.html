The Nordic Region includes five countries: Denmark, Norway, Sweden, Finland, and Iceland. Tables comparing the Nordic country, as well as the averages from the Nordic region and global averages for each score. Note that the human freedom score is a composite score of the personal and economic freedom scores.

nordic_averages = summarise_all(nordic_only, mean)

global_averages = summarise_all(merged_df, mean, na.rm = TRUE)

## logical: returning NA

global_averages[is.na(global_averages)] = 1
                          
nordic_data = 
  rbind(nordic_only, nordic_averages, global_averages) %>% 
  mutate(
    countries = as.character(countries),
    countries = 
      str_replace(countries, "0", "nordic_average"),
    countries = 
      str_replace(countries, "1", "global_average")
    )
